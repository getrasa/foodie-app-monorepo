# FeedbackBite — Project Description

## Mission

Help restaurant owners collect genuine customer feedback by rewarding diners with instant discount codes — while organically encouraging Google Maps reviews without violating any platform policies.

---

## The Problem

Restaurant owners need Google reviews to attract new customers, but they have no compliant way to incentivise them. Offering rewards for Google reviews directly violates Google's policies and can result in listing suspension. Meanwhile, valuable customer feedback goes uncollected because there's no simple, frictionless mechanism at the point of dining.

## The Solution

FeedbackBite gives restaurant owners a simple tool: a QR code they place on tables, receipts, or walls. Diners scan it, land on a quick feedback page, leave a star rating and optional comment, and instantly receive a discount code for their next visit. After receiving their discount, they see a gentle, optional nudge to also share their experience on Google Maps. The discount is tied to feedback on our platform — never to the Google review — keeping everything policy-compliant.

---

## How It Works

### For the Restaurant Owner

1. Signs up and creates their restaurant profile
2. Configures their discount offer (percentage off, fixed amount, or a free item like "free dessert")
3. Downloads a QR code and places it in their restaurant
4. Views incoming feedback and basic analytics from a simple web dashboard
5. Redeems discount codes when diners return

### For the Diner

1. Scans the QR code at the table — no app download, no account creation
2. Sees a clean, branded feedback page with the restaurant's name and logo
3. Taps a star rating (1–5) and optionally writes a comment
4. Instantly receives a discount code on screen with clear instructions: "Show this to your server on your next visit"
5. Below the discount, sees a soft optional nudge: "Enjoyed your meal? Share it on Google too!" with a link to the restaurant's Google Maps review page
6. The nudge is clearly separate from the discount — no strings attached

---

## Owner Dashboard

The owner has a simple web-based console where they can:

- **See feedback at a glance** — total count, average rating, recent comments
- **Read individual feedback entries** — star rating, comment, date
- **View basic analytics** — rating trends over time, rating distribution, discount redemption rate
- **Manage their discount offer** — change the type, value, or expiry, pause it temporarily
- **Download their QR code** — in printable formats ready for table tents, receipts, or stickers
- **Redeem discount codes** — enter a code to verify and mark it as used when a diner returns

---

## Key Principles

**Frictionless for diners.** No app download. No account. No login. Scan, tap, done. The entire feedback flow should take under 60 seconds.

**Policy-compliant.** The discount is a reward for leaving feedback on our platform. The Google review nudge is presented separately, after the discount is already granted, and clearly framed as optional. No reward is tied to posting a Google review.

**Simple for owners.** Onboarding should take under 3 minutes. The dashboard shows what matters — feedback, ratings, discount activity — without overwhelming with data.

**Abuse-resistant.** The system prevents the same person from farming discount codes through device fingerprinting, rate limiting, and optional daily caps set by the owner.

## MVP Scope

### Included
- Owner registration and login
- Single restaurant per owner
- Restaurant profile with Google Maps integration for place identification
- Discount configuration (percentage off, fixed amount, or free item)
- QR code generation and download
- Public mobile-optimised feedback page for diners (no login required)
- Star rating and optional comment submission
- Instant discount code generation and display
- Google review nudge (optional, decoupled from discount)
- Owner dashboard with feedback list, basic analytics, and discount code redemption
- Abuse prevention

### Explicitly Excluded from MVP
- Multiple restaurants per owner
- Diner accounts or login
- SMS or email delivery of discount codes
- Sentiment analysis
- Custom branding beyond logo
- Multi-language support
- Subscription or billing (free during validation)
- Native mobile apps
- Staff sub-accounts
- POS integrations

---

## Success Criteria

- An owner can go from signup to QR code on the table in under 3 minutes
- A diner can go from scan to discount code in under 60 seconds
- The owner sees actionable feedback appearing in their dashboard in real time
- Google review nudge is presented naturally without creating policy risk
- The system prevents obvious abuse without adding friction for legitimate diners
